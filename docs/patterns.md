


Allowed patterns to play/stop:
* - {patternname}
* - ~off
* - ~blink-{color}-{count}
* - ~blink-{color}-{count}-{on/off time}
* - ~blink-white-3
* - ~blink-#ff00ff-5
* - ~blink-green-3-0.5
* - ~pattern:3,#ff00ff,0.5,0,#00ff00,1.3,0
* - ~pattern:1,#00f300,0.5,0,#000000,0.5,0
* - ~pattern-stop:pattname
* - #hexcolor
